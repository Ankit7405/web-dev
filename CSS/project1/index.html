Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya (1 cup)
  • Maida (2 tbsp)
  • Milk (2-3 tbsp)
  • Baking soda (a pinch)
  • Ghee (for frying)
  • Sugar Syrup

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar and water until slightly sticky; add cardamom, rose water, and saffron.
  2. Mix khoya, maida, baking soda, and milk into a soft dough.
  3. Roll into smooth, crack-free balls.
  4. Deep fry on low heat until golden brown.
  5. Add to warm syrup and let soak for 1-2 hours.
